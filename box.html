IDEA

The Operating System for Offline Retention

The "Phygital" Bridge

By using digital engagement we can track and reward physical shopping behaviour that currently leaves no data trace

Payment-Agnostic Rewards

Reward system should work regardless of payment via cash, card, MFS, or QR

The Data Layer

By identifying repeat buyers, we can move away from generic discounts to personalized, data-driven retention

Stackable Value

Yeamazing rewards should be "stackable," meaning they should work on top of existing store-specific promotions so the consumer never has to choose between two benefits

DIFFERENTIATION

Why This Model is "Hard to Copy"

Frictionless User Experience

Unlike traditional loyalty programs that require physical cards or complex sign-ups at the counter, our solution offers "instant gratification" with cashback sent directly to a mobile wallet

Zero Behavior Change

While a bank app only rewards its own users, Yeamazing will reward everyone, making it more inclusive for the 18-35 tech-savvy demographic

Merchant-Centric Design

Our solution will not require expensive hardware or complex software training, as a result it will scale faster than competitors

Strategic Constraints as Moats

By staying away from being a bank app, a delivery service, or an online marketplace, we avoid the high-burn competition of those sectors and dominate the "offline blindspot"
